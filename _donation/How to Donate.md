<h4>HOW TO DONATE</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/About/header-border.png">
</div>
<p>Since 1981, the National Crime Prevention Council (NCPC), a non-profit
organisation committed to rallying crime prevention efforts and awareness
in Singapore, has been carrying out public education activities and series
of initiatives to raise public vigilance against crime; NCPC also strongly
advocates active citizenry in the fight against crime.</p>
<p>Crime prevention is a shared responsibility for everyone and NCPC seeks
your generous contributions to support the cause of crime prevention! No
amount of contribution is too small for our cause and every dollar of your
contribution will go a long way towards making your home and nation safer
and more secure for all.</p>
<p>We also accord strong recognition to all our valued donors - “Platinum
Donors”, “Gold Donors” and Silver Donors”.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/chart-donation.JPG">
</div>
<p>So join us in our fight against crime now! Simply fill up the <a href="https://appdev.ifdemo.com/ncpcorgsg/downloads/NCPC-Donation-Form.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">donation form </a>here
and make cheques payable to the “National Crime Prevention Council”.</p>
<p>You may also donate to us via the <a href="https://www.giving.sg/national-crime-prevention-council" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Giving.sg platform</a> to
fund our crime prevention programmes.</p>
