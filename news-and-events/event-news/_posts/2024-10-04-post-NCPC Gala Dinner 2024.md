<p>On 27 September 2024, the National Crime Prevention Council (NCPC) held
our Fundraising Gala Dinner at Marina Bay Sands, graced by our Guest of
Honour, Mr. K. Shanmugam, Minister for Home Affairs and Minister for Law.
With over 400 distinguished guests, including donors, corporate partners,
and community stakeholders, the evening marked a key milestone in the fight
against scams with the launch of the ScamShield Suite.</p>
<p>The ScamShield Suite now encompasses various anti-scam resources such
as:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>24/7 ScamShield Helpline (1799)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ScamShield App</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ScamShield Website (www.scamshield.gov.sg)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ScamShield Alert Channels on WhatsApp &amp; Telegram</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>

<p>We’re proud to announce that the evening raised over $500,000 to support
NCPC’s programmes. Thank you to all our generous donors and partners for
your unwavering support in keeping Singapore safe and secure!</p>
