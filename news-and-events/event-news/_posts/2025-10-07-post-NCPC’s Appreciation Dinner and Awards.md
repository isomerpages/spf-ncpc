<p>NCPC’s Appreciation Dinner and Awards was held on 7 October 2025 at the
Singapore Marriott Tang Plaza Hotel, graced by Guest of Honour, Mr. Goh
Pei Ming, Minister of State for Ministry of Home Affairs and Ministry of
Social and Family Development. With close to 300 distinguished guests,
including donors, corporate partners, and community stakeholders, the evening
celebrated our partners’ efforts towards crime prevention with the launch
of the inaugural NCPC Crime Prevention Awards.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Events/NCPC_Appreication_Dinner_and_Awards_2.jpg">

<p>The Award is a new initiative to recognise organisations that have taken
proactive steps to prevent crime, working hand in hand with the Council
to safeguard Singapore and keep communities safe. A total of 10 organisations
were honoured this year. Four companies were awarded the ‘Champion’ award
for their outstanding leadership and excellence in crime prevention, while
six received the ‘Partner’ award for their meaningful contributions in
support of safer communities:</p>
