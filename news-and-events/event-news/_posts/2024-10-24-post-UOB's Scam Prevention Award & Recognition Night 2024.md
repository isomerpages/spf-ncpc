<p>UOB's Scam Prevention Award &amp; Recognition Night 2024 celebrated UOB’s
branch heroes and partners for their exceptional work in deterring scams.

Appreciation Award to Mr Tan Puay Kern, Vice Chairman of NCPC, in recognition
of the strong collaboration between UOB and NCPC in various anti-scam initiatives.</p>
