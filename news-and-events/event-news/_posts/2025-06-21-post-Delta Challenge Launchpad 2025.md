<p>On 21 June 2025, the National Crime Prevention Council (NCPC) and the
Singapore Police Force jointly organised the Delta Challenge Launchpad
2025.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Events/Picture1.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>The Delta Challenge Launchpad provided youths with a valuable opportunity
to engage with experts on crime related issues. Through interactive sessions
and discussions, participants gained deeper insights into these crimes
and explored practical strategies to combat them.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Guest speakers also shared valuable insights on the two challenge tracks,
highlighting key concerns and the issues they aim to address.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Events/P6210370.jpg">
</div>
<p>NCPC Council Members Mr Nicholas Khoo, Dr Hana Alhadad, and Ms Sherrin
Chua participated in the Delta Challenge Panel Discussion, engaging directly
with youth participants, answering their questions, and encouraging them
to take an active role in building a safer community.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Delta Challenge comprises two exciting tracks:</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/NCPC_Delta_Challenge_MasterKVEdited_3Jun.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Track A: Art Against Scams</strong> – Unleash your creativity for
a cause! Submit your mural design to help raise awareness about scams and
stand a chance to win up to $10,000. Selected designs will be transformed
into public murals displayed across Singapore – turning art into a powerful
tool for education.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Track B: Guardians of Tomorrow</strong> – Got a bold idea? Share
your innovative concepts addressing key crime prevention themes. Selected
entries may receive a grant of up to $5,000 to turn your vision into reality
and create meaningful impact in the community.</p>
